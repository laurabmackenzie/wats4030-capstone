Never forget where you want to go.

We’ve all seen the headlines:
Top 10 places to see before you die.
20 places to go off the beaten path.
Cheapest holiday destinations in Europe.
Here’s the problem. We don’t read these articles when we’re planning our vacation. We read them at Starbucks. We read them in the grocery line. We read them at the dentist’s office. Then, much later, it’s time to plan our next vacation. Okay, now, what was that magical undiscovered paradise? Where exactly was I supposed to go before I die? Enter Wanderlist, an app that allows you to quickly jot down the places that you want to go and view them on a map. Your list will always be there, so you don’t forget when it’s actually time to go. If you’ve got wanderlust, you’re going to need a Wanderlist.
